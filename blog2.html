My Journey in Tech and Data (so far)

Today, I want to share with you my journey - a journey that has taken me from the fundamentals of Computer Science in India to the world of Big Data in Waterloo, and now into the depths of Business Analytics in Toronto. It's a story about passion, discovery, and the evolution of a career in technology and data.


How it all began

My adventure began with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in India. I was drawn to the field by my fascination with technology and the transformative power of programming. Those years were filled with learning the intricacies of algorithms, the structure of programming languages, and the rigor of computational thinking. This period laid the foundational stones of my technical skills and prepared me for the complex world of technology.


A Turn Towards Data – Big Data Solutions Architecture in Waterloo

As my undergraduate days were nearing their end, I stumbled upon an intriguing realization - the importance and power of data. This realization was a turning point. It led me to pursue a post-graduate diploma in Big Data Solutions Architecture in Waterloo. Here, I delved deep into data science, exploring its vast potentials and applications. This experience was eye-opening; I discovered my passion for data and its ability to drive decisions and innovation. But more importantly, it helped me realize that my interests lie not just in coding but in the intersection of technology, business, and finance.


Blending Business with Analytics – A New Direction in Toronto

This newfound interest led me to Toronto, where I embarked on my second post-graduate program in Analytics to Business Decision Making. This program is not just another academic pursuit; it is a journey into the world of business research, development, and even statistics. After years of deep diving into coding, this transition felt refreshing and exhilarating. It's fascinating to see how data analytics can be applied to solve real-world business problems, offering insights that can drive strategic decisions.


The Refreshing Transition and Current Experiences

The shift from a purely technical focus to a more business-oriented approach has been a refreshing experience. Every day is a new opportunity to learn and grow. I am enjoying every bit of it – from understanding market trends to analyzing consumer behavior and even predicting business outcomes using statistical models. It's a whole new world where numbers tell stories and insights lead to strategies.


Looking Ahead...

As I continue on this path, my goal is to become an exceptional Business Analyst. I envision a career where I can blend my technical expertise in data with my growing knowledge of business dynamics to make impactful decisions. The journey has been full of learning and self-discovery, and I eagerly look forward to what the future holds.


This journey from a Computer Science graduate to an aspiring Business Analyst has taught me the importance of continuous learning and adaptability. It's a path that has shown me the power of data beyond just coding – as a tool for business insights and decision-making. I'm excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and am determined to make the most of them. Stay tuned as I continue to explore this fascinating intersection of technology, data, and business!
